ABOUT TALENTZ


Talentz Project Expo aims at bringing out the innovative thoughts with inspiring ideas that ignite the young minds. This expo helps in displaying the projects developed by the students as part of their academic activity, thus recognising their work.



OBJECTIVES


To exhibit the skills of the students and to showcase their innovation to the world.
