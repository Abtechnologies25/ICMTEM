Best Paper Award

★Best Presentation Award will be given for a presentation that stands out among the other presentations, taking into consideration all conference presenters who are highly professional, inspiring, and impactful in their delivery of their material.

★The Conference Chair selects the winning presentation based on the evaluation committee's scores for each presentation as well as his or her own perusal of the papers.




Session’s Best Award

★The session chair selects which presentation will win the Session's Best Presentation Award based on the ratings provided by the evaluation committee, which consists of the session chair and two members of the evaluation panel for each presentation of each session.

★The Best Student Presentation award will be granted for an outstanding conference presentation in order to recognise the excellent work done by student participants. The recipient of the award must be enrolled as a student in order to be eligible for the award.

★The prize is given out in accordance with a strict criteria of quality, and all student presenters will be given consideration for it.

★The conference chair selects which presentation will win the Best Research Paper Award according to the assessment committee's grades for each student presenter's presentation and his or her personal review of the papers.
